Our Origin

The Story Behind
VetJobs Portal

After years of watching talented veterans return home only to face long job searches, underemployment, or roles that didn't match their capabilities it became clear something was missing.

Skills proven in high-pressure environments discipline, leadership, problem-solving under uncertainty were often invisible on paper or misunderstood in interviews. Employment gaps that didn't reflect ability. Civilian roles that felt foreign. Employers who genuinely wanted to hire veterans but didn't understand military roles in resume.

VetJobPortal was created to close that gap not as another job board, but as a purposeful bridge that helps veterans clearly show their value and helps employers confidently bring that talent onboard.

Service doesn't end. It simply changes uniforms.

The Problem

A Gap Nobody
Was Solving

Every year, thousands of Nigerian military veterans leave service with world class discipline, leadership, and technical skills. Most of them cannot find work that matches what they are worth. That is not a veteran problem. That is a systems problem.

3,000+

Nigerian military personnel retire or are discharged every year entering a civilian job market with no structured transition support.

Zero

Before VetJobPortal, there was no dedicated structure built specifically for Nigerian or African military veterans employment.

Invisible

Military CVs are written in a language civilian employers don't understand. Skills go unseen. Careers stall. Experience gets wasted.

The System Was
Never Built For Them

Generic job boards were built for generic candidates. Veterans are not generic. They operate in high-stakes environments, lead teams under pressure, and execute with precision qualities that most civilian employers desperately need but rarely seen on a standard CV.

The result is a painful mismatch employers struggling to find reliable, disciplined talent while thousands of battle-tested professionals sit underemployed or unemployed, their potential completely invisible to the market.

  • No platform to translate military roles into civilian job titles
  • No employer database actively seeking veteran talent in Nigeria
  • No mentorship or transition support after discharge
  • No community connecting veterans to each other or to opportunity
  • VetJobPortal was built to fix every single one of these gaps starting in Nigeria, scaling across Africa.
